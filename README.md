## FeedForward: Logistics for Social Good

**FeedForward** is a micro-SaaS platform designed to bridge the gap between food surplus and community need. By connecting **restaurants** directly with **local NGOs**, the platform automates the redirection of high-quality leftovers to those who need them most, reducing urban waste while nourishing vulnerable populations.

### 🚀 Key Features
* **Real-Time Donation Logging:** Restaurants can quickly list surplus meals, ingredients, or baked goods.
* **Unique Tracking System:** Generates custom IDs (e.g., `FF-XXXXXX`) to monitor the lifecycle of a donation from listing to delivery.
* **NGO Matching:** Facilitates seamless coordination for pickups and schedules.
* **Impact Transparency:** A public leaderboard highlights top "Community Hero" restaurants to encourage corporate social responsibility.
* **Dynamic Status Updates:** Visual timeline for tracking "NGO Accepted," "Out for Pickup," and "Delivered" stages.

### 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** Semantic HTML5, CSS3 (with custom animations), and Vanilla JavaScript.
* **Data Management:** LocalStorage for persistent tracking and state management.
* **Design:** Mobile-responsive UI with a focus on high-stakes utility and scannability.
